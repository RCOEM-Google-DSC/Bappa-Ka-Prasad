About

Competitive Coding Contest

This event will be organised under Google DSC RCOEM Chapter on the Occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi under the name Bappa ka Prashad. This event will help the students to develop their coding skills and clear their doubts from their seniors. It will be a fun activity contest with lots of learning. The main objective of the event is to help the students getting started with competitive programming and give them a introduction to various CP platforms in a fun and easy way possible.
